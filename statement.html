Artist Statement

In the 1990's, I sat myself down in from the television and consumed a large quantity of cartoons. The quirky and anthropomorphic imagery imbedded itself in my mind. In more recent times my midwestern upbringing and my occupation as a bartender has kept me quite occupied with beer. I began to work these two influences together through wood and paint. Working with wood on the lathe allows me to mill bottles and cans with precision. I then alter the form by hand to bring these fantasy objects to life. Painting allows me to use contorted perspectives and infuse strange narratives that reflect on some of the common midwestern experiences. The following works are the formalities of art, altered with my own dark humor, under influence of 90's cartoons.
